Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Trending News Generator?

Our AI Trending News Generator is a tool that scans various online sources to identify the latest trending news and topics. It then generates concise summaries or overviews of these trends, helping you stay informed quickly.

How fresh is the news it generates?

The tool is designed to fetch information from current and rapidly updating sources, ensuring that the news summaries you receive are as fresh and relevant as possible to the trending topics.

Can I filter news by specific topics or categories?

Yes, our tool allows you to provide keywords or select specific categories (like Technology, Business, Health, etc.) to narrow down the news results. This allows you to focus on the trends most relevant to your interests or industry.
