Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI generate a story?

You provide a topic, genre, and main characters. The AI uses this information as a prompt, drawing on its vast knowledge of narrative structures, character archetypes, and plot devices to weave a unique story for you.

Can I get a full-length novel?

Currently, the tool is optimized for generating short stories or story starters. It's excellent for sparking ideas and overcoming writer's block, but not for writing a complete novel in a single go.

Is the content original?

Yes, each story is generated uniquely based on your specific inputs. The AI creates new text and does not copy from existing sources, so the content is original.
