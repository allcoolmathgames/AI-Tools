Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Summarizer.AI par, hum aapki privacy ka ehtram karte hain. Yeh Privacy Policy batati hai ke hum aapki information kaise collect, use, aur protect karte hain jab aap hamari website aur services (jo ke `summarizer.org` par dastyab hain) ka istemal karte hain.

2. Information We Collect

Hum sirf woh information collect karte hain jo hamari services ko behtar banane aur unhein operate karne ke liye zaroori hai:

  • **Usage Data:** Hum non-personally identifiable usage data collect kar sakte hain, jaise ke aap kaunse tools istemal karte hain, kitni baar, aur technical information jaise browser type, device type. Yeh data hamari services ko improve karne ke liye anonymous tareeqe se analyze kiya jata hai.
  • **Content Data:** Jab aap hamare tools mein text input karte hain (maslan summarization ke liye), to woh text sirf processing ke liye istemal hota hai aur foran delete kar diya jata hai. Hum aapka input kiya gaya content save ya store nahin karte.

3. How We Use Your Information

Hum aapki collect ki gayi information ka istemal is maqsad ke liye karte hain:

  • Apni services ko provide, maintain, aur improve karna.
  • Usage patterns ko analyze karna taake hamare tools ko behtar banaya ja sake.
  • Technical maslon ko identify aur resolve karna.

4. Data Security

Hum aapki information ko unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, aur destruction se bachane ke liye munasib security measures (jaise encryption) ka istemal karte hain. Choonke hum koi sensitive personal data store nahi karte, aapki privacy mazeed secure rehti hai.

5. Third-Party Links

Hamari website par doosri websites ke links ho sakte hain. Hum un websites ki privacy practices ke zimmedar nahi hain. Hum aapko hidayat karte hain ke aap kisi bhi third-party site par visit karne se pehle unki Privacy Policies ko parhen.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Hum waqt-fawaqt apni Privacy Policy ko update kar sakte hain. Jab hum koi changes karenge to hum is page par "Last Updated" date ko tabdeel kar denge. Hum aapko encourage karte hain ke aap periodically is Policy ko review karte rahen.
