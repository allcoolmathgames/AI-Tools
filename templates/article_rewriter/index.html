Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Article Rewriter?

An AI Article Rewriter is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to rephrase text, sentences, or entire articles while maintaining the original meaning. It helps in creating unique content and avoiding plagiarism.

How does it ensure uniqueness?

The AI analyzes the input text and uses a vast vocabulary and different sentence structures to generate a new version. This process ensures that the rewritten content is distinct from the original while conveying the same message.

Can I adjust the rewriting style?

Yes, our tool often provides a "creativity level" slider. This allows you to control how much the AI rephrases the text, ranging from subtle changes to more significant rewrites, giving you flexibility over the output style.
