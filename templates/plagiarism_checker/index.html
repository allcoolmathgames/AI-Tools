Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Plagiarism Checker?

An AI Plagiarism Checker is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to scan your text and compare it against a vast database of web pages, academic papers, and other documents to detect any instances of copied or unoriginal content.

Can it detect AI-generated content?

Yes, in addition to traditional plagiarism, our tool is designed to identify patterns commonly found in AI-generated text, helping you determine if content was created by an AI model rather than a human writer.

Is it suitable for academic use?

Absolutely. This tool is ideal for students, educators, and researchers to ensure academic integrity by checking essays, theses, and papers for both unintentional plagiarism and AI-generated content before submission.
