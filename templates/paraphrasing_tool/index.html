Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI Paraphrasing Tool?

An AI Paraphrasing Tool uses artificial intelligence to rephrase sentences, paragraphs, or entire articles. It helps you express ideas in new ways, improve clarity, expand vocabulary, and avoid accidental plagiarism while preserving the original meaning.

How does it help avoid plagiarism?

By generating unique versions of existing text, the tool helps ensure that your content is original. It rephrases the input using different wording and sentence structures, making it distinct from the source while conveying the same information.

Can I use this for academic writing?

Yes, it's a useful tool for academic writing to help you understand and articulate information from sources in your own words. However, always ensure proper citation of original sources even after paraphrasing to maintain academic integrity.
